About Me

Capability

As a first year MSI student at University of Michigan, I learn research and design methods and tools, including contextual interviews, user testing, brainstorming, affinity mapping, prototyping, wireframing, graphic design, with tools include Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, InVision, Axure, HTML, CSS, and python.

Philosophy

As a user driven designer, I believe all kinds of "process"-including administration, production, communication, etc.-can be improved by design. Making improvement and solving problem is my goal as a designer. Design is the solution. And the key to the solution is listening. When people complain, I listen and see the chance.

Personality

I am a easy-going, responsible, and organized person. As a previous administrative manager working at the Office of International Affairs, National Taiwan University, my work experience in serving international students make me open-minded and embrace all the differences.
