About Me

Positive and Reflective. I love people, people, and people. Nothing is more interesting than people's stories.

Capability

I majored in information science and specialized in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan. I learned how to do UX research and design, developed know-hows on user research, interaction design, pervasive design, design validation and prototyping at school. After interning at Citrix System Inc. as a Product Design Research intern, I am capable to lead, operate and execute UX research study solely. This winter, I am going back to Citrix for my second internship.

Philosophy

Design is the solution. And the key to the solution is Research. How users use technology is most important. I believe all kinds of "process"-including administration, production, communication, etc.-can be improved by design. Making improvement and solving problems is my goal. When people complain, I listen and see the chance.

Personality

I am an easy-going, responsible, and organized person. I enjoy working both individually and on a team. I always like to balanced individual efforts and team works. Furthermore, I love to work and make things work out while being goofy. Humor and laughing are two things should never disappear in my life :)

This is me. And yes, I love sky diving!
