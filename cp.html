Consultant Project

January-Present, 2017

-This is my story about Solving communication problems between organizations.-

L program is a non-profit organization that cooperates frequently with M program. In 2014, the programatic structure changed and led to different copperations between the two. They encountered messy communication in past two years, and thus asks for helps for the solution.

To comply with my confidential agreement, I have changed the names of the organizations and people's names. If there is any same name you know, it is just a coincidence. Confidential information in this case study will not be revealed.

My Roles

Skill Set: Project Management | Research | Interview | Report Writing | Presentation

I voluntarily work on this project individually. I cooperate with the clients and will conduct 6 interviews in total. So far I have conducted 1 interview with the director of L program.

Approaches

Overarching Question: How to Improve the Communication between L program and M program?

  • Background Research

    To understand the backdround about the two organizations, I first do research on the history of the two institutes. And then I tackle the problem by searching for communication tools and common internal communication problems. This approach helps me perpare the interview portocols and have the background knowledge before conduting interviews.

  • Contextual Interviews

    I have talked to one member in the L program and quickly found that there are several painpoints from the person's perspective. I took affinity notes and generated more follow up questions on the interview portocols for other interviewees.
