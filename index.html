Hi, I am Amy

I am a User Experience Researcher actively seeking for a full-time position. If you need any help related to UX, welcome to email to yinganc@umich.edu and let's start the conversation!

Why research?

Research helps keep focusing on users. With research, empathy has a solid base, and so does the design itself. Without research, we work with illusion and imagination. Want to have an impact? Then do research.

What do I do?

I guarantee users are at the center. I identify design problems, improve solutions, and assist design iteration. I complete the puzzle in the design process and validate all the hypothesis that people may wonder.

How do I do it?

I talk and listen. I ask how and note down the process; I probe why and compose the stories behind. I think hard, dig deep, and verify with analysis and reflection. I apply empathy and love any user feedback wholeheartedly.

Capability

I am a UX researcher with 2-year UX research experience as an HCI student, a product design research intern, and a UX research contractor. I am capable to lead, operate and execute UX research study solely. I am familiar with interaction design and ubiquitous computing research and design process. I help test and validate assumptions, and build information architecture, wireframing and prototyping. I can assure the users are positioned at the center of the design process.

Philosophy

I do research because I don't like to waste materials and people's time. By doing research, I can assure the design meets the needs. Design is the solution, but the key to the solution is research. How users use technology is most important. I believe there is neither "stupid user" nor "bad design," but only miss-matching of the two. Making improvement and solving problems are my goals. When people complain, I listen and see the chance.
