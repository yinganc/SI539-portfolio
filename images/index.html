Hi, I am Amy

Currently, I am a UX research contractor at Citrix, actively seeking a full-time position. If you need any help related to UX, welcome to email to yinganc@umich.edu and let's talk!

Why research?

Research helps keep focusing on users. With research, empathy has a solid base, and so does the design itself. Without research, we work with illusion and imagination. I want to have an impact, so I do research.

What do I do?

I guarantee users are at the center. I identify needs and problems, improve design solutions, and assist design iteration. I complete the puzzle in the design process and validate all the assumptions that people may wonder.

How do I do it?

I talk and listen. I ask how and note down the process; I probe why and compose the stories behind. I think hard, dig deep, and verify with analysis and reflection. I apply empathy and love any user feedback.

About Me

I am a 2-year Master's student focusing on Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Michigan, School of Information. I regard myself a User Experience Researcher and Advocator. People use "Curious, Deep, and Reflective" to describe me. I love to be with people, talk to people, and listen to people. Nothing can be more interesting than people and their stories behind.

Capability

I am a User Experience Researcher with 2-year UX research experience. I am capable to lead, operate and execute UX research study on my own. I am familiar with different research methodologies and have experience in interaction design and ubiquitous computing. I help find the users needs, test and validate design concepts and assumptions, and build information architecture, wireframing and prototyping. I can assure the users are positioned at the center of the design process.

Philosophy

I do research because I believe the impact it brings. You can say I am skeptical because I am. I don't believe the problem can be solved when no one knows what the problem is. As a researcher, I help people to see the truth and understand deeper. By doing research, I can assure the design meets the needs. I believe there is neither "stupid user" nor "bad design," but only miss-matching of the two. Making improvement and solving problems are my goals. When people complain, I listen and see the chance.

Personality

I am a reflective, easy-going, and also hyper person. I love to think harder and dig deeper when there comes a question. As a team player, I am a responsible, organized, and people-oriented person. And I enjoy working both individually and on a team. I always like to balance individual efforts and team works. Furthermore, I love to work and make things work out while being goofy. Humor and laughing are two things will and shall never disappear in my life :)
