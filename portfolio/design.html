SOCHI

  • Topic: Homeless Youth Education

    • Team members: Jenny, Stephanie, Asha, Aakanksha, and me
    • Purpose: To assist homeless children to access formal education
    • Process: We interviewed the related organizations, including the county district office, non-profit organization, and the shelters to gather information regarding the issue.
    • Solution: We are still trying to figure out the main need. And the process of finding solution is still on-going.

OpenAir

  • Topic: Website Accessibility

    • Team members: Michael, Stephanie, Meg, Nalingna, and me
    • Purpose: To assist the non-profit organization to improve the accessibility of their website
    • Process: We discussed with the non-profit organization online and did the training provided by Knowbility in order to learn how to build a website that is accessible to everyone.
    • Final: We were required to use WordPress as a tool to build the website for SUMMIT IDG.
